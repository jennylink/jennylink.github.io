IDEA

During my work in the print facilities of my former Universities, I always felt bad about the waste of materials. Especially the amount of paper waste you get, when setting up a job on industrial printing machines.
I believe that, even though the quality of the prints is not good enough for the assigned job - it is still to good to throw it away. Therefore I started to collect misprinted papers and cut leftovers to reuse them for cover material. The result were unique, little notebooks containing of different, leftover papers. Perfect as little gifts!
