TERRIBLE PEOPLE #2

CULTURE // POLITICS // TECHNOLOGY & SHIT


IF YOU HAVE EVER WONDERED WHY PEOPLE DO SOME STUPID THINGS, SO HAVE WE...

Terrible People is a satirical magazine which aims to display the ‘dark sides’ of human nature in a funny way. It's main objectives are to open up people’s minds to new ways of thinking and to make them reflect on their own behaviour while still being entertaining and amusing. The magazine features articles on politics, lifestyle topics, and the peculiarities of different cultures, as well as photo features and illustrations.
