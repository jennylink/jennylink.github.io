IDEA

During the work of some projects in the print facilities of my former Uni I always felt sorry for the waste of paper. Especially the amount of paper waste you get, when setting up a job on industrial printing machines.
Even though the quality of the prints was not good enough for the assigned project - it was still to good for me to throw it in the bin. Therefore I started collecting printed papers from diferent jobs, that were declared as paper waste and used them as cover paper for notebooks.
The result were beautful little notebooks with random covers and different papers. Beautifully perfect as little gifts!
